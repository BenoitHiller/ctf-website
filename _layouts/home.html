Who we are

CTF is a student-run group, started 11 years ago with three computers. We manage computers nad printing in Burnside, paid for by the 21st century fund by science students. Find out more about us on the about page.

Our services

We offer the cheapest black-and-white printing on campus, at 4 cents a page, and manage over 100 computers, both in Burnside basement and in several remote stations. We also host events - check out the events page for more.
